Introduction

Welcome to Ava AI ("we," "our," or "us"). This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website, use the Ava AI application, or interact with our services on the Axiom Pro platform on Solana.

This policy provides you with clear and comprehensive information about the cookies we use, how they work, and how you can control them. By continuing to use our services, you are agreeing to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies help us recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your user preferences, settings, and previous activities. This enables us to enhance your experience with our services and provide you with personalized features.

In addition to cookies, we may use other similar technologies, such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage, which serve similar purposes. For simplicity, we refer to all these technologies collectively as "cookies" in this policy.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our platform:

Cookie Type Description Duration
Essential Cookies Necessary for the platform to function properly. They enable core features such as security, network management, and account access. Session / Persistent
Functional Cookies Enhance your experience by remembering your preferences and settings, such as language preference and display settings. Persistent (1 year)
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how you interact with our platform, allowing us to improve functionality and user experience. Persistent (2 years)
Performance Cookies Collect information about how you use our platform, such as which pages you visit most frequently. Persistent (90 days)
Trading Interaction Cookies Remember your trading preferences and interaction patterns to optimize Ava AI's voice and text responses. Persistent (6 months)

Your Cookie Choices

You have the right to choose whether to accept or reject cookies. Essential cookies cannot be rejected as they are necessary for the proper functioning of our platform.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser.
  • Our Cookie Banner: When you first visit our platform, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to select which types of cookies you accept.
  • Cookie Preference Center: You can update your cookie preferences at any time through our Cookie Preference Center.
  • Opt-Out Links: For third-party cookies, you can use the opt-out links provided by the respective third parties.

Please note that rejecting certain cookies may impact your experience with our platform, as some features may not function properly.

Browser-Specific Instructions

To manage cookies in your specific browser:

Chrome

  1. Click on the menu icon in the top right corner
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click on "Privacy and security"
  4. Click on "Cookies and other site data"
  5. Adjust your cookie settings

Firefox

  1. Click on the menu icon in the top right corner
  2. Select "Options" (Windows) or "Preferences" (Mac)
  3. Select "Privacy & Security"
  4. Navigate to the "Cookies and Site Data" section
  5. Adjust your cookie settings

Safari

  1. Click on "Safari" in the menu bar
  2. Select "Preferences"
  3. Click on the "Privacy" tab
  4. Adjust your cookie settings

Edge

  1. Click on the menu icon in the top right corner
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click on "Cookies and site permissions"
  4. Navigate to "Manage and delete cookies and site data"
  5. Adjust your cookie settings

Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that signals to websites that you do not want your online activities tracked. Due to the lack of a common understanding of how to interpret these signals, our platform currently does not respond to "Do Not Track" signals. However, you can control the use of cookies as described in this policy.
